Spicy Shrimp Tacos

These spicy shrimp tacos are packed with flavor and perfect for any occasion. They're quick and easy to make, making them a great option for weeknight dinners or entertaining guests.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, toss shrimp with olive oil, chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Add shrimp and cook for 2-3 minutes per side, until pink and cooked through.
  3. Warm tortillas in the skillet or microwave.
  4. Assemble tacos by filling each tortilla with shrimp, shredded cabbage, avocado slices, and cilantro.
  5. Serve tacos with lime wedges on the side.
